Kangaroo Island

Wingara is a self contained Beach House retreat with breathtaking, uninterrupted sea views, over looking a natural boat basin and invesigator straight to the Althorpe Islands.

Situated on the water's edge, you can marvel at the abundance of local wildlife including Kangaroos, wallabies, dolphins, pelicans, seals and much more all form your private deck.
